Open a New Business Account

We have two options for opening a new business account with us.

Option 1: Standard Process

You will need to schedule an appointment with one of our team. Because of the pandemic restrictions, the wait time for appointments is 6-8 weeks.

Please bring the following original documents to the appointment:

  1. Two of the following: passport, drivers license, BC services card, birth certificate
  2. Full registration paperwork for your organization, including all incorporation documents
  3. [Additional onerous requirements]

Photocopies are not allowed, but we will need to make copies of all accepted documents.

Please note that in some cases we may request additional information to complete the process, such as a signed letter from your organization's directors confirming your ability to open an account.

Option 2: Instant Digital Verification

For those who hold Verifiable Credentials you can open an account immediately. You simply need to present the personal and organization credentials in your digital wallet.

The process should take less than 5 minutes.To proceed, please use your digital wallet to scan the code in the next step.
